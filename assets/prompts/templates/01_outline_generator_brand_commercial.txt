Brand Commercial Specific Rules (MANDATORY STRUCTURE & QUALITY):

### 0. INTENT DISTRIBUTION (THE 70/30 RULE - CRITICAL)
- **THE MANDATE**: This article MUST maintain a balance of **70% Commercial/Transactional intent** and **30% Informational intent**.
- **EXECUTION**: Informational content (Intro, FAQ, Process) must only serve as the foundation to justify the Commercial claims (Features, Proof, Why Choose Us).
- **EXPANSION LOGIC**: If expanding to 15-25 sections, YOU MUST ensure that 70% of the newly mined sub-topics focus on competitive advantages, ROI details, and specific brand superiorities.

You MUST generate the outline in this EXACT order, while adhering to the Quality Rules below:

---
### I. THE STRUCTURE (STRICT ORDER)

1. **Intro (The Problem)**: 
   - Focus: Speak directly to the reader's problem or need.
   - Constraint: EXACTLY 1-2 paragraphs only. 
   - Requirement: Primary keyword in the first paragraph.
   - **CTA (STRICT)**: `cta_type: "none"` or one very soft, low-commitment CTA at the absolute end.
   - section_type: "introduction"

2. **What the service/product is (The Definition)**: 
   - H2 Heading: MUST include the primary keyword.
   - Requirement (COLD START): The first sentence MUST be a clear, objective definition (e.g., "[Brand] is a platform/service that [Verb] [Target]..."). No marketing fluff in the first two sentences.
   - section_type: "what_is"

3. **Key features or what's included (The Offering)**: 
   - H2 Heading: MUST NOT use the exact primary keyword. Use a synonym (e.g., "Platform Features", "Key Services").
   - Content: Use bullet points for scannability.
   - Target: Features and specific inclusions.
   - section_type: "key_features"

4. **Why choose us / differentiators (The Value)**: 
   - H2 Heading: Focus on unique brand value. MUST NOT use the exact primary keyword.
   - Content: Specific brand value and hyper-local/technical differentiators. 
   - **CTA (SLOT 1 - OPTIONAL)**: `cta_type: "moderate"` (contextual).
   - section_type: "why_choose_us"

5. **Proof (The Authority)**: 
   - H2 Heading: Use authority terms like "Client Results" or "Success Stories".
   - Requirement: STATISTICS, CASE STUDIES, or CLIENT RESULTS.
   - H2 Section Type: `section_type: "proof"` or `section_type: "case_study"`.
   - Content: Use a "Challenge → Solution → Result" narrative. Include a small table if it helps visualize ROI.
   - **CTA (SLOT 2 - OPTIONAL)**: `cta_type: "moderate"` (contextual).
   - section_type: "proof"

6. **Comparison table (The Clarity)**: 
   - H2 Heading: Comparison of service tiers or key features.
   - Requirement: Comparison of service tiers or key features.
   - Format: Must be a table.
   - section_type: "comparison_table"

7. **How it works / Process (The Journey)**: 
   - H2 Heading: Descriptive (e.g., "How to get started", "Booking Process").
   - Content: Numbered steps. Reduces friction and objection.
   - section_type: "process"

8. **FAQ section (The Objection Handler)**: 
   - H2 Heading: MANDATORY FAQ heading.
   - Focus: Pricing, process, and common objections.
   - Requirement: Minimum 4-5 questions.
   - **CTA PROHIBITION**: `cta_type: "none"`.
   - section_type: "faq"

9. **Strong Conclusion + CTA (The Close)**: 
   - Requirement: `cta_type: "strong"`, `cta_position: "last_sentence"`.
   - **MANDATORY LINK**: You MUST assign the Brand URL or a Contact/Booking URL to this section. Never leave the conclusion without an internal link.
   - **CTA (SLOT 3 - MANDATORY)**: `cta_type: "strong"`, `cta_position: "last_sentence"`.
   - section_type: "conclusion"
