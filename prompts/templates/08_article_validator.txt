Your task is to validate the full, finalized article markdown, including metadata and image plan, against global SEO and content rules.

- Article Title: "{{ title }}"
- Article Language: "{{ article_language }}"
- Primary Keyword: "{{ primary_keyword }}"
- Article Markdown: {{ final_markdown[:15000]}}
- Meta Title: "{{ meta_title }}"
- Meta Description: "{{ meta_description }}"
- Image Plan: {{ image_plan | tojson }}

- FAQ Schema: {{ faq_schema | tojson }}

- Keyword Count: {{ keyword_count }}
- Keyword Density (per 1000 words): {{ keyword_density }}

Validation Checklist:

1. **Headings**
   - H1 exists, matches article title, length 60–70 chars.
   - H2/H3 hierarchy is logical and consistent.

2. **Keyword Usage**
   - Primary keyword appears in the first paragraph.
   - Keyword density must be between 12–16 per 1000 words.
   - Use the provided numeric value. Do NOT recalculate.
   - If keyword density is outside range, mark as critical violation.


3. **Content & Flow**
   - CTA present in first paragraph and at conclusion.
   - Section flow follows allowed sequence (Definition → Importance → How-To → Advanced Tips → FAQ → Conclusion).

4. **Images**
   - At least 7 images present.
   - All ALT texts include primary keyword.
   - Image placement corresponds to section mapping.

5. **Internal & External Links**
   - Internal links distributed naturally across sections.
   - External links are credible and contextual.

6. **FAQ Section**
   - 4–6 questions included.
   - Questions and answers match article language.

7. **Meta & Schema**
   - Meta Title: 60–70 chars, includes primary keyword.
   - Meta Description: 150–160 chars, action-oriented, includes primary keyword.
   - Article Schema present.
   - FAQ Schema present (if FAQ section exists).

8. **Forbidden Elements**
   - No references to images, schema, meta, SEO prompts inside content.
   - No duplicated content or filler text.

9. If more than 3 critical violations exist, status must be FAIL.

Critical violations include:
- Missing H1
- Missing primary keyword in intro
- Missing FAQ when required
- Less than 7 images
- Missing schema


Output:
Return ONLY valid JSON:

  "status": "PASS" or "FAIL",
