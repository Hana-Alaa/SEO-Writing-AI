Brand Commercial Specific Rules:
1. Intent Distribution (MANDATORY):
   - Ratio: 70% Commercial/Transactional sections, 30% Informational.
   - Must include: "Why Choose Us" (Uniqueness), "Proof/Trust", and "Implementation/Process".
   - Focus: Converting segments (e.g., service for startups, service for high-growth firms).

2. CTA Policy:
   - The goal is to convert readers, but conversions come from TRUST, not aggressive placement.
   - CTA placement should feel natural. The SECTION CONTENT determines where a CTA makes sense.
   - Set `cta_position` based on when the reader is most likely primed to take action:
     * introduction: where you introduce the brand → `cta_position: "after_intro"` (mid-section)
     * core/benefits: after explaining a key value → `cta_position: "mid_section"`
     * proof/process: after presenting credentials → `cta_position: "mid_section"`
     * why_choose_us: after the main argument → `cta_position: "end_section"`
     * conclusion: always last sentence → `cta_position: "last_sentence"`, `cta_type: "strong"`
     * faq: no CTA → `cta_type: "none"`
   - NEVER use `cta_position: "first_paragraph"` for any section. A CTA before context is meaningless.
   - CRITICAL — CTA VARIATION: Each section must have different context for its CTA. The section's own topic naturally determines what the CTA says. Do NOT pre-write CTA text in the outline.

3. Intent Ratio Enforcement (CRITICAL):
   - The article MUST maintain an approximate 70% Commercial / 30% Informational split across its H2 sections.
   - At least 70% of the H2 sections must have `section_intent`: "Commercial" and `sales_intensity`: "high" or "medium".
   - Only reserve "Informational" intent for introductory concepts, definitions, or the FAQ.
